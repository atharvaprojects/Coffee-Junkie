House Blend Coffee

House Blend coffee is a medium-roast blend, known for its smooth, well-balanced flavor with mild acidity and subtle sweetness. It typically combines high-quality Latin American beans, offering rich, nutty, and chocolatey notes. With a clean, crisp finish and a hint of caramel, it's a versatile, everyday coffee enjoyed black or with milk, perfect for any time of day.

French Roast

French Roast coffee is a dark, intense roast with bold, smoky flavors and low acidity. It features a rich, bittersweet taste with caramelized notes, often highlighting a deep, charred aroma. The oils on the beans create a velvety mouthfeel, making it a favorite for those who enjoy a strong, full-bodied coffee with a slightly burnt undertone.
